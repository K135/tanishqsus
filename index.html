Responsible Neighborhood

Titan has over a period of time taken up many lakes or water bodies in the vicinity of its manufacturing locations at Hosur/Pantnagar and rejuvenating them making sure a few water bodies such as ponds are restored to enable water conservation. The biggest initiative has been rainwater harvesting across all plant locations..
